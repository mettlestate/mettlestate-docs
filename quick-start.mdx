## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- **Modern Web Browser** - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Internet Connection** - Stable connection for optimal performance
- **Email Address** - Valid email for account verification
- **Gaming Account** - Optional but recommended for full experience

## Step 1: Create Your Account

### 1.1 Navigate to Registration

- Visit the [Mettlestate Platform](https://mettlestate.com)
- Click **"Get Started"**
- Or go directly to [Register](https://mettlestate.com/register)

### 1.2 Fill Out Registration Form

- **Username**: Choose a unique username (max 16 characters)
- **Email**: Enter your email address
- **Password**: Create a strong password
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

### 1.3 Verify Your Email

- Check your email for verification link
- Click the verification link to activate your account
- Return to the platform to complete setup

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

### 2.1 Gaming Preferences

- **Favorite Games**: Select games you play

### 2.2 Basic Information

- **Personal information:** Add in your personal information (name, birthday, contact details, gender, country)
- **Profile Picture**: Upload a profile image (optional)
- **Bio**: Brief description about yourself (optional)

### 2.3 Privacy Settings

- **Contact Preferences**: Set communication preferences

## Step 3: Explore the Platform

### 3.1 Platform Overview

- **Upcoming Events**: View tournaments and events
- **Recent Activity**: See your recent matches and achievements
- **Quick Actions**: Access common features

### 3.2 Navigation Menu

- **Home**: Main dashboard and overview
- **Events**: Browse all tournaments and events
- **Battlegrounds**: Quick competitive matches with leaderboards
- **Battle Pass**: Seasonal progression system
- **Wallet**: Manage your prize money
- **Profile**: Account and settings

## Step 4: Join Your First Tournament

### 4.1 Browse Available Events

- Navigate to **Events** section
- Filter by game, date, or tournament type
- Read event details and rules

### 4.2 Register for Event

- Click **"Register"** on desired event
- Review registration requirements
- Confirm participation
- View the [Events Section](/events/overview) for a more in-depth how to

### 4.3 Prepare for Competition

- Check event schedule and rules
- Ensure game client is updated
- Test your connection and setup

## Step 5: Participate in Matches

### 5.1 Match Notifications

- Receive match notifications via email and on the Mettlestate platform
- Check match details and timing
- Join game lobby when ready

### 5.2 Game Session

- Enter lobby code and password
- Connect with opponents
- Play your match according to rules

### 5.3 Result Submission

- Submit match results
- Upload screenshots if required
- Wait for result verification

## Essential Features to Know

### Battle Pass System

- **Free Tier**: Basic progression and rewards
- **Premium Tier**: Enhanced rewards and content
- **Missions**: Complete challenges for XP
- **Seasonal Content**: New content each season

### Team Management

- **Create Teams**: Form competitive teams
- **Invite Members**: Add players to your team
- **Team Operations**: Manage roster and settings
- **Team Challenges**: Issue challenges to other teams

### Financial Features

- **Wallet System**: Manage tournament winnings
- **Prize Distribution**: Automatic prize allocation
- **Withdrawals**: Cash out your earnings
- **Transaction History**: Track all financial activity
