Event registration is the process of registering for an event.

## Registration Types
- Solo Registration
- With a Team

## Registration Process

## Player How-To
- Browse Events and open the event you want to join
- Click Register and choose Solo or Team (where applicable)
- Complete eligibility requirements (game account linking, age, region, etc.)
- Confirm registration and check your dashboard for match times

## Admin Capabilities
- Configure registration windows and capacity/waitlist rules
- Define eligibility checks (linked accounts, age/region restrictions)
- Approve/deny registrations and promote from waitlists
- View audit logs for late adds, removals, and changes
