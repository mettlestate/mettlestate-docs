# Organizations

Hierarchical management for multi-team organizations, with permissions and oversight across teams and players.

![Placeholder](/images/screenshots/organizations-overview.png)
*Replace with actual diagram/screenshot*

## Key Capabilities
- Manage teams and members at scale.
- Assign roles and permissions.
- Oversee finance (prizes, wallets).

## Why it matters
Structured controls enable semi-pro and pro orgs to operate efficiently.

## Admin Actions
- Configure organization roles and permissions.
- Review and approve organization-level finance actions.
- Support org managers with cross-team roster issues.
- Send announcements to organization managers.
