Our software allows users to create and manage teams with roles (captain, player, reserve, coach, manager). Users can also coordinate rosters for participation across games and events. Users are able to manage members and scheduling.

<img src="/images/screenshots/teams-overview.png" alt="Teams Overview" />

## How it works

- Invitations and approvals
- Custom branding and social links
- Team chat/coordination for matches and schedules
