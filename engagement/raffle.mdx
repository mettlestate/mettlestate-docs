Themed raffles with prizes. Each raffle can have a different theme and prizes. Raffles are linked to the Battle Pass timeline, raffles are held at the end of each Battle Pass season.

<Icon icon="link" size={18} /> [Raffle](https://mettlestate.com/raffle) (Link to raffle page)

## Seasonal Progression
- Earn XP from matches and missions to level up
- Unlock cosmetics, entries, and perks
- Optional premium track with enhanced rewards
