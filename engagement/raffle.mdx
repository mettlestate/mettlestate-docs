Mettlestate has built a Raffles rewards system. Each Raffle can have a different theme and prizes. Raffles are linked to the Battle Pass timeline, and are held at the end of each Battle Pass season.

<Icon icon="link" size={18} /> [Raffle](https://mettlestate.com/raffle)

## Raffle Tickets

Earn Raffle tickets through:

- Battle Pass progression
- Purchasing with Mettallions
- Winning through crates
